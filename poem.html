I think like the rain makes me unique. Am I a tortured artist yet?

blood photography

there is a cemetery filled with sacred possessions
memories that nightmares fear to awaken
momentary impulses that desecrated holy lands

to cope with existing in this nonsensical world
i snap chains and let wolves howl; a photo session

i spray heart's ichor on the pyre of pain
where smoke dances with aurora
corpses run around in free politic
exorcised of all humane constraints
works of shakespeare set ablaze

sacrifice to god within me; monster of my brain
that consumes fire; drives me away from sagacity
past and future ousts present, i possess myself
thousand sensations; iron in ears, bells in mouth
buried in the graveyard, burning under the pyre

not dead, but on ice with lucifer
resurrecting life’s punishments
the taste of her impish smile

my mind sprouts from my head and blooms
frozen flowers, remnants of drifting psyche
fuel hell’s furnace; melt me into corruption
searing remnants of ice, guide to flawed truth

when i awake, a forest in ashes, blind parishioners weep
next to me, on pristine paper, a single thought

fooled once

exposing my soul to bond with another
beautiful facade for twisted sickness

my back is open, sharpen your knife
i have blindfolded myself, come closer

blade between the shoulders
laughter of smiling masks
friendship bad punchline to worse joke

a natural disaster

massive slabs beneath our feet
pull and tear rock apart
liquifying stable ground

but they promised me i would be protected
so i rebuilt myself above torn up ground
and as soon as i laid down foundation
they strained and smashed to create calamities

volcanoes burn and crush my house
earthquakes rips my body in half
tsunamis dowse my hearth, swallow all

i plummet into a deep crevice
a sun hidden behind rain clouds
is the last remains of my vision
before the mantle claims me

like all things in life,
the rocks will swim into the ocean
to them, i fade into a distant dream

i remain, rain soaked and dirt smeared
stuck under the entire weight of earth
each breath supplying lungs with gravel
faint outlines are all vision can supply
the fridgidness masking the loneliness
last connection to reality, tips of tree roots

how did i not see this coming
what folly grabbed my mind
to think i could control nature

hunger

the tiniest nibble of stale crackers
cannonballs exploding on tongue
ephemeral orgasms; a heartbeat
evocation of a completed puzzle
festering wounds turned baby skin
my ancient civilization restored

mouth salivating, drool frothing
all that matters is this glimmer
medicinal herbs for crushed souls
inebriating drinks for torrid innards

desperation drives a maddening search
the next great calamity; mass extinction
capitals to ghost towns, rainforests to deserts
all prosperity devoured off the planet

now, i am one with vultures and rats
scavenging suburbia for giddy euphoria
diving into dumpsters when footsteps clap
scrambling back to my windowless box
consumed by all matters of depraved
my body transformed to glut; doughy sweat
endlessly bulldozing garbage heaps into me
lusting for the next needle, bag, bottle
two chalices, poison and antidote; identical
highest peaks turned hopeless baselines
i am chasing bliss to barrel bottom

fireflies in a mason jar

i wish the fireflies would stop hitting the sides of the mason jar
pernicious hysteria, twisted monstrosities
desperate to burn their impressions in me
blinking lights; breaking all focus, blocking all progress
i thought their lives short, but they seem determined to stay

chittering, screaming, blinking, darting
infesting every corner of my mind
smearing my thoughts mahogany and crimson
they slam at my skull when i try to remove them
twisting and pulling helplessly, the immobile lid

gouge my eyes out, yet i see them
rip my nose off, yet i smell them
stab my ears, yet i hear them
set my skin ablaze, yet i feel them
sew my mouth shut, yet i taste them

they are everything

the other bugs, bits of peace providing brief respite
now litter the bottom of the mason jar as rot
but the fucking fireflies remain
guess i have to break the jar

nothing really matters

walking forward, back turned to the future
all the time to look back
ridicule every failure
sparse success, hollow happiness
hate myself more for it

rage takes over
i see those who wronged me, those who created me
i will spite them, cut off my face until i am nothing
they will never know

i trip on something i can not see, slicing my arm
red oil spurts everywhere, coating my surroundings
another avoidable scar, another piece to justify pain within

the landscape close to me is barren; dead trees in blood sand
reminder of all opportunities to live wasting away

shoes ruined, clothes in tatters
stomach roars with non-existent hunger
once, people drove by, begging me to change course
new clothes, the smallest sip of water
finally, they just screamed to turn around

a few months ago, my parents passed
they hardly tapped the brakes
i almost feared they were gone forever

the sun rears from behind clouds smothering it
it burns my eyes, and i flinch forwards
infinitesimal second

truths carved in stone becomes puffs of air
time stops; i know not what i am
broken, starved; empty body collapses

ode to death, a dear friend

dark side of the mind

poisoned drus killed god

playboy bunny fantasies

the darkest beast

a tale dedicated to a shell and the great humanity that never was

hiding in distractions

gift

frustrated

the demise of fools

currents seventeen

the boy's room
